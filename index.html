Hi, my name is Katarina Roberts. I am a certified UX Desinger in Aberdeen, Scotland!

I am able to connect with the product/service users on their level, due to empathy skills that I have developed in my previous career as a Freelance artist and also as a carer. I pay attention to the detail and I like to gain perspective from the user point of view to gain a better understanding of the problem I am trying to solve.

My work

Experte | 2019

Experte is a web based application. Experte’s main aim is to provide expert advice when help is needed. Our app aims to be engaging and provide rewarding and meaningful interactions in an honest and consistent way to both the Experts and Advice Seekers. The app has been designed with a growth perspective, always trying to improve our user’s experience.

Duolang | 2018

Is an app with aim to empower people learn new vocabulary. As part of this project I have gone through the basics of the design process to design a basic low-fidelity prototype of the application. The application allows user to learn vocabulary in a fun way, going through different games, improving their statistics and share and compare scores.
